This monorepo contains the following projects:

- apps/highland-hosts-admin-website
  - Used only by Website administrator for the purpose of website admin, booking management and actions, analytics/reporting.

- apps/highland-hosts-api
  - Handles authentication, property management, bookings, payments, post-booking actions

- apps/highland-hosts-guest-website
  - Marketing website aimed towards people looking for accomodation (BnB specifically) on the Isle of Skye.
  - Aim to attract users searching for BnB's on seach engines (not glamping)
  - Provides many BnB listings from various hosts (up to 1000), availability and enables booking requests and payment.

- apps/skye-glamping-website
  - Marketing website aimed at customers looking for accomodation (Glamping specifically) on the Isle of Skye.
  - Aim to attract users seraching for 'glamping' on seach engines (not BnB)
  - Offers a handful of listings all from a single host at the same property location.
- apps/highland-hosts-app
  - Solely aimed at the hosts of listings that are available on highland-hosts-guest-website
  - Includes capability for hosts to manage their listing, configure preferences of payments, listing pricing etc.
