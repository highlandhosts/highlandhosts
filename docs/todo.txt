Notes about copying AirBNB

You cannot copy:
  Visual layouts that are highly distinctive
  Icons, illustrations, photos
  Exact wording
  Screen designs that are “substantially similar”
  Function is OK. Expression is protected.
You can't market as "AirBNB but cheaper"
TLDR:
  - Don't use exact wording
  - Diverge layout/style slightly where possible
