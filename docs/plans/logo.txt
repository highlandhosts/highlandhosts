🧭 HighlandHosts Logo Specification (Refined Concept)
🧱 1. Overall Layout (Very Important)

Shape: Circular badge (clean, balanced)

   [ Top: Mountains / sky (minimal) ]

   [ Center: CABIN (main focus) ]

   [ Left: Host (Male) Waving with right arm
   [ Right: Host (Female) Waving with right arm]

   -------------------------------
   |        RIBBON (bottom)      |
   |  Highland Hosts             |
   |  Book local & save on stays |
   -------------------------------

👉 Key rule:
Top = visual story
Bottom = brand + message
❌ No elements below the ribbon

🏠 2. Cabin (Primary Focus – ~70% visual weight)

Style:

Traditional Scottish Highland cottage
Whitewashed or stone walls
Dark slate roof
Chimney with visible smoke
Warm glowing windows (cosy feel)

Details to include:

Slightly asymmetrical stonework (authentic feel)
Small porch or wooden door
Soft warm light (#E9B949 whisky gold tone)

👉 This should be the first thing your eye goes to

🧍‍♂️ 3. Host Figures (Secondary – ~30%)

Style:

Friendly Highland host (not cartoonish)
Subtle tartan sash (not overpowering)
Pose: Enthusiaast, welcoming wave. It must not simliar to a heil hitler gesture AT ALL
CRITICAL FIXES:

Eyes perfectly aligned (symmetrical)
Man has long but not unkempt ginger hair and a thick but smart beard.
Woman has a tam hat and looks like a hearty scottish lass. (Not overly good looking but pleasant, kind face)
Expression: warm, very welcoming, natural, overly animated

👉 Think: “trustworthy local host”, not mascot

🎗 6. Ribbon (Bottom Placement Only)

Position:

Fully below main illustration
Slight curve, but not too dramatic

Content (all centered)

Highland Hosts
Motto: 
"Book local" Then new line, then "Save on stays"
Both lines of motto should use same font and weight

Typography:

Top line: bold serif (clear, strong)
Bottom line: smaller, simpler

Color:

Background: highlandMossGreen (#5F7F4B)
Text: driftwoodSand (#E7E1D6)
Accent: whiskyGold (#E9B949) for subtle trim

Highland wilfflowers stick out of top of ribbon, including some from heatherPurple = '#8B6FAF';
👉 Must remain readable at small sizes

🎨 7. Colour Usage (Keep Tight)

Stick mainly to:

Deep Skye Blue (#1F3F4A) – outlines / depth
Moss Green (#5F7F4B) – ribbon / trees
Warm Stone / Sand – cabin + text contrast
Whisky Gold – window glow + highlights

👉 Avoid overusing purple/red—keep it natural

🔍 8. Scalability Rule (Super Important)

At 50px × 50px, the logo should still:

Clearly show:
Cabin shape
Circular badge
Host becomes simplified silhouette
Cow may reduce to shape or disappear

👉 If details don’t survive at 50px → remove/simplify

🧠 9. Hidden Pro Move (Optional but Powerful)

Design a secondary icon version:

Just:
Cabin + chimney smoke
Subtle “H” shape in structure

👉 This becomes:

App icon
Favicon
Mobile header logo
🏆 Final Design Priorities (in order)
Cabin clarity & warmth
Clean, readable structure
Friendly but not cartoonish host
Minimal, supportive cow
Strong, legible ribbon text
